Why Physics?

Because physics is interesting

Physics helps us to understand how the world around us works, from can openers, light bulbs and cell phones to muscles, lungs and brains; from paints, piccolos and pirouettes to cameras, cars and planes; from earthquakes, tsunamis and hurricanes to quarks, DNA and black holes.
