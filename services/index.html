Software Engineering

We’re experts at web and mobile apps development. We reduce development risk for our clients by using sprint methodology and standards-based code to bring digital platforms to market quickly. We staff each engineering engagement with an optimized team that typically includes a Technical Architect, a mix of full-stack and mobile engineers, UX/UI designer and a dedicated Project Manager. We are believers in modern DevOps as a way to incorporate an integrated code lifecycle.

Innovation Workshops

Webloon Studio Innovation Workshops are a 5-10 day process we’ve developed for getting from ideation through to product definition. We usually start every new client engagement at Webloon Studio with one of these. Our approach is designed to shortcut usual debate cycles and get rapid clarity and consensus on what we are building – and why. We typically emerge from the Workshop with actual proof-of-concept prototype, ready to go directly into the engineering phase.

Accelerator     Assets

To accelerate development of certain applications, we have written our own reusable software assets upon which custom applications can reside. Our product allows us to more quickly and efficiently deploy a wide range of solutions requiring native mobile forms. Microsoft addresses a broad set of field sales use cases, while Social9 gives us freedom for unlimited content sharing of Internet of Things. By building web solutions on top of our existing IP, we can get clients to market faster, while reducing engineering costs.
